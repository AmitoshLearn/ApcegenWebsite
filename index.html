About Us

Pioneering affordable human therapeutics.

APCEGEN is an innovation-driven biopharmaceutical company developing affordable human therapeutics through advances in molecular and cell biology. We are committed to helping millions worldwide in the fight against cancer, rheumatoid arthritis, diabetes, and other life-threatening diseases.

On the nanotechnology front, APCEGEN is advancing nanofiber-based cardiovascular tissue engineering. Our group focuses on pathway engineering and proprietary drug development platforms, with a substantial emphasis on the technologies that bridge discovery research and large-scale biopharmaceutical manufacturing.

Our state-of-the-art research platforms address the key challenges of both early- and late-stage product development — from cell line development through to commercial production processes and global regulatory compliance.

Core Capabilities

A vertically integrated biopharma stack.

From cell line to clinic. Our research platforms address every stage of biopharmaceutical product development with quality aligned to global regulatory standards.

01 — Platforms

Cell Line Development

A novel mammalian expression vector for high-level expression of heterologous proteins and antibodies in CHO cells. Combined with our two-step selection process to deliver high-expression stable clones. We have also developed an in-house microbial expression vector for periplasmic expression of recombinant proteins and antibodies.

02 — Process

Process Development

Bioprocess development built on Quality-by-Design principles. Upstream and downstream optimization for early- and late-stage programs, with seamless scale-up and tech transfer pathways from R&D bench to GMP-ready manufacturing.

03 — Engineering

Antibody Engineering

Our scientists are constantly developing novel antibody formats — bispecifics, antibody-drug conjugates, and next-generation therapeutic constructs designed for improved efficacy, half-life, and tissue targeting.

04 — Quality

Pharma Analytics & QC

Comprehensive analytical development covering release testing, characterization, and stability. Methods aligned to ICH, USP, and EP guidelines to support regulatory submissions and commercial launch readiness.

Autoimmune Disorders

Translating pathogenesis into targeted therapies. Our autoimmune programs focus on rheumatoid arthritis and related inflammatory conditions, with disease-modifying mechanisms that go beyond symptom relief.

Oncology & Immuno-Oncology

Our objective is to reverse the cancer mortality trend. We focus on immuno-oncology — harnessing the body's own immune system to target solid tumors with monoclonal antibodies and engineered constructs.
